The Best Movies According to Nadja

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favorite childhood movie. Some parts scared me as a kid but I grew to love it!

Midsommar

A very unique thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat. The visuals are beautiful and I love the character development.

Alice in Wonderland

I have always loved the book and the Disney movie always gives me nostalgia.
